Best Abandoned Cart Tools for Shopify

Abandoned cart recovery tools help Shopify stores recover lost revenue by sending automated email or SMS reminders when customers leave without completing checkout.

Quick picks

  • Omnisend – Best email + SMS automation
  • Klaviyo – Advanced marketing automation
  • LiveRecover – Human-powered SMS recovery

FAQs

How many carts are abandoned? Often 60%+—recovery flows are essential.

Does SMS outperform email? SMS usually gets faster opens and higher conversions.
