Omnisend vs Klaviyo for Shopify Cart Recovery

Omnisend is great for fast setup and affordable automation. Klaviyo excels at advanced segmentation and analytics for data-driven marketing.

Quick picks

  • Omnisend – Best for simple automation
  • Klaviyo – Best for advanced marketing automation
  • LiveRecover – Best human SMS recovery

FAQs

Which recovers more carts? Depends on your flows; both can perform very well.

Is SMS worth it? Yes—typically faster reads and higher conversions.
