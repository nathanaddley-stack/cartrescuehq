Best AI Chatbots for Shopify Customer Service

AI chatbots reduce repetitive support tickets by automatically answering common Shopify questions such as order status, shipping updates, and return policies. The best tools integrate directly with Shopify data and messaging channels.

• Tidio – Best AI chatbot + live chat combo
• Zendesk AI – Enterprise-grade automation
• Gorgias AI – Shopify-native support bot

• Q: Can chatbots reduce support tickets?
A: Yes—many bots resolve repetitive questions such as order tracking automatically.
• Q: What channels do Shopify chatbots support?
A: Web chat, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, and sometimes WhatsApp.

A: Many start with low-tier plans suitable for small stores.
