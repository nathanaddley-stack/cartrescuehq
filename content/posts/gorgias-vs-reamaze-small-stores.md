Gorgias vs Re:amaze for Shopify (Small Stores)

Gorgias and Re:amaze are two of the most common helpdesk platforms used by Shopify merchants. Both centralize customer conversations but differ in automation depth and pricing.

Quick picks

• Gorgias – Best Shopify integration
• Re:amaze – Best affordable helpdesk
• Zendesk – Enterprise alternative

Comparison table

| Product   | Best for             | Key features              | Integrations | Pricing*    | Pros                  | Cons                 |
| --------- | -------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------ | ----------- | --------------------- | -------------------- |
| Gorgias   | Shopify-native teams | Order actions, automation | Shopify, IG  | Tiered      | Deep Shopify features | Costly               |
| Re:amaze  | SMB helpdesk         | Chat + ticketing          | Shopify      | Lower tiers | Affordable            | Fewer automations    |
| Zendesk   | Large support teams  | Workflow automation       | Many         | Enterprise  | Scalable              | Complex              |
| Tidio     | Chat-first support   | AI bot + chat             | Shopify      | Low start   | Easy setup            | Limited helpdesk     |
| Freshdesk | General helpdesk     | Ticket automation         | Many         | Mid tiers   | Flexible              | Not Shopify-specific |

*Note pricing as of 2026-03-13; verify on vendor site.

When to choose Gorgias vs Re:amaze

Choose Gorgias if you want deep Shopify integrations such as editing orders from tickets.
Choose Re:amaze if you want a simpler and more affordable helpdesk for a small team.

FAQs

• Q: Which is better for Shopify?
A: Gorgias has deeper Shopify integrations.
• Q: Is Re:amaze cheaper?
A: Yes, Re:amaze typically has lower pricing tiers.
• Q: Can both handle social messages?
A: Yes—both support channels like email and social messaging.
